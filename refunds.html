Glamour Nail Studio Refund Policy

We value our customers and strive to provide the best service possible. Please read our refund policy carefully before making a booking.

Refund Policy Overview:

  • Booking deposits are **non-refundable** but can be rescheduled within 48 hours of the original appointment.
  • If you are unsatisfied with your nails, please notify us **within 24 hours** for a complimentary touch-up.
  • We do not offer refunds for completed services, but we will ensure you are happy with the final result.
  • In cases of allergic reactions or medical concerns, please contact us, and we will assess the situation individually.

Late Cancellations & No-Shows:

  • Cancellations within **24 hours** of the appointment will result in a **50% charge** of the full service.
  • No-shows will forfeit the deposit and may be required to pay in full for future bookings.
